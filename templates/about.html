About Me

Hello! I’m a pre-final year engineering student with a strong passion for software development, innovation, and building solutions that matter.

I've actively participated in national-level hackathons and tech competitions, and I’m proud to have won the Best Societal Patent award at a hackathon. I currently have two more patents in progress, focused on addressing real-world challenges through scalable tech.

Beyond competitions, I’ve taken part in several workshops and seminars, including Manthan, Cyber Security, which broadened my understanding of cutting-edge technologies and industry expectations.

What drives me is the opportunity to combine creativity with code. I love collaborating in teams, thinking critically under pressure, and turning raw ideas into working products. I’m especially passionate about full-stack development, clean tech, and ethical software systems.

Looking ahead, I aspire to grow into a well-rounded software developer who not only builds robust applications but also contributes to societal progress through meaningful innovations.
